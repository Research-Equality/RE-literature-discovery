# Findings Template

Use this file as a running synthesis note for long-running literature reviews.

It is not the final survey or Related Work section. It is the evolving internal understanding that bridges phase outputs and later writing.

## Research Question

One clear sentence describing the review question.

## Current Understanding

What does the corpus currently suggest? Write this as a revisable synthesis, not a final claim.

## Key Results

List the strongest evidence or recurring patterns from the papers reviewed so far.

## Patterns and Insights

What themes, taxonomies, or causal explanations are emerging across papers?

## Lessons and Constraints

What should constrain future searching, filtering, or synthesis?

Examples:

- a subtopic is oversaturated and should be de-prioritized
- a benchmark dominates the field and distorts comparison quality
- a claimed novelty repeatedly turns out to be only a scope shift

## Open Questions

What remains unresolved, contradictory, or weakly evidenced?

## Writing Handoff

Track which downstream artifact should consume these findings next:

- `literature-review`
- `cross-paper-synthesis`
- `related-work-writing`
- `survey-generation`
